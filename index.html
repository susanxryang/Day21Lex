About
US sex education mainly focuses on physical aspects of sexual development and overlooks the emotional aspects of psycho-secual development. As a result, many lack the understanding and communication skills to protect themselves and develop healthy relationships.
To adddress this, we wanted to record and re-enact deeply personal and relatable stories and events that reflect the struggles of teenagers and people newly entering relationships. Thus we created an interactive storytelling web-based game with resources to help users understand conflicts and issues in relationships from an outside perspective and learn to mitigate these situations with more knowledge and better resources.

Relationship Stories

Four journeys demonstrating real-life issues occurring in relationships.

Interactive Choice Making

Education

Useful external articles and resources to help users learn different topics in building healthy relationships.
